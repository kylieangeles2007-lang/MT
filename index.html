How to avoid losing track

Every peso needs a trail

  1. Set the correct starting balance for Cash, GoTyme, Go Save, and bank.
  2. When you pay with cash, record it as an expense from Cash.
  3. When you send InstaPay to someone as payment, record it as an expense from the wallet/bank used.
  4. When you move your own money, like Cash to GoTyme, GoTyme to Go Save, or Go Save back to Cash, record it as a transfer, not an expense.
  5. If the app balance does not match real life, use Set actual balance in Accounts. The app will record the correction so you know there was a mismatch.
